Open Positions Describtion

Hadoti Mahila Kisan Producer Company Limited (Hadoti MKPCL), Kota-Baran (Rajasthan), a Producer Company being incubated by FDRVC and supported under National Rural Livelihoods Mission, is offering unique opportunities to those who have the passion to work with rural poor and willing take up the challenge to work for the small and marginal farmers.

The Producer Company will be dealing in coriander, soybean and other agriculture produce involving procurement, processing and its bulk, retail packaging/consumer packs by investing in large scale processing units and marketing of the commodity and the value-added products. It is going to be working with a large farmer base and will have modern equipment for commodity handling and IT based business processes. FDRVC has been retained by Hadoti MKPCL to hire for the position and requires qualified candidates for the following positions.
